Letter from Kichi

A few words I never said out loud

If you are reading this, then maybe you have already walked through my story. Or maybe you are still somewhere in the beginning, trying to understand it.

There are things I never knew how to say directly. Not because they were too big… but because they felt too soft to break into words.

Sometimes I wonder how two people can feel so much without ever putting it into sentences. How silence can become a language only they understand.

I don’t know what this story becomes in the end. But I know what it felt like while living it. And that feeling… was enough.

— Kichi
